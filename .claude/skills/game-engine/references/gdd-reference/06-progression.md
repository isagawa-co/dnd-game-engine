# 6. Progression — Tiny Civ Reference

## Tech Tree

### Structure
Techs organized into eras. Each has science cost and prerequisites. Tree branches and converges.

### Eras and Key Techs
- **Ancient** — Agriculture, Mining, Pottery, Writing, Archery, Bronze Working, Wheel, Masonry, Mysticism, Animal Husbandry
- **Classical** — Philosophy, Mathematics, Currency, Construction, Iron Working, Horseback Riding, Calendar, Engineering
- **Medieval** — Theology, Education, Chivalry, Machinery, Civil Service, Guilds, Code of Laws
- **Renaissance** — Gunpowder, Printing, Banking, Astronomy, Navigation, Drama
- **Industrial** — Industrialization, Rifling, Metallurgy, Military Science, Economics, Fertilizer, Steam Power
- **Modern** — Replaceable Parts, Combustion, Ballistics, Electricity, Radio, Flight, Medicine, Atomic Theory

### Research
- Science per turn = sum of all cities' science output
- Cost scales per era: Ancient ~20, Classical ~50, Medieval ~100, Renaissance ~200, Industrial ~400, Modern ~800
- First civ to research a tech → announced to all

## Civic Policies
Culture fills policy meter. 6 trees, 5 policies each:

| Tree | Focus | Playstyle |
|------|-------|-----------|
| Tradition | Capital + small empire | Tall play |
| Liberty | Expansion, settlers, workers | Wide play |
| Honor | Combat, XP, maintenance | Military |
| Piety | Faith, religious spread | Religion |
| Commerce | Gold, trade, purchasing | Economy |
| Rationalism | Research, specialists | Science |

## Governments
One active at a time. Switching costs 3 turns anarchy (no yields).

| Government | Requires | Bonus | Penalty |
|------------|----------|-------|---------|
| Chiefdom | Default | None | -25% science |
| Monarchy | Code of Laws | +2 gold/city, +1 happiness/luxury | — |
| Republic | Philosophy | +1 prod/city, +5% science | -1 military maintenance |
| Theocracy | Theology | +25% faith, buy military with faith | -10% science |
| Democracy | Printing | +25% science, +25% gold | Can't declare war 10 turns |
| Autocracy | Industrialization | +25% prod, +25% combat | -2 happiness/city |
| Communism | Economics | No building maintenance | -50% trade gold |

## Religion

### Founding
- 20 faith → Pantheon (minor passive bonus)
- 60 faith → Religion (name + 2 beliefs)
- Limited religions: civs / 2, minimum 2. First come, first served.

### Pantheons
| Pantheon | Effect |
|----------|--------|
| Sun God | +1 food from wheat/cattle |
| Stone Circles | +2 faith from quarries |
| God of the Sea | +1 production from fishing boats |
| Fertility Rites | +10% growth all cities |
| Monument to the Gods | +15% production toward wonders |
| God of War | +faith from kills |

### Beliefs (choose 2)
| Belief | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| Tithe | +1 gold per 4 followers |
| Church Property | +2 gold per city following |
| Religious Community | +1% production per follower (max 15%) |
| Defender of the Faith | +20% combat near friendly cities |
| Holy Warriors | Purchase military with faith |
| Missionary Zeal | Missionaries +25% spread |
| Sacred Sites | +2 culture from shrines/temples |
| Papal Primacy | +15 influence with city-states |

### Spread
- Passive through trade routes and proximity
- Missionaries actively convert (2 charges)
- City religion = whichever has most followers
